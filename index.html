RE-USING CANDLES

Extend the life of your candles by repurposing the wax remnants into new ones or using the containers for various purposes, promoting sustainability. Reusing candles reduces waste and adds a touch of creativity to your decor..

HOW TO PICK THE RIGHT SCENT

Select a scent that aligns with your mood or purpose; calming lavender for relaxation or invigorating citrus for energy. Additionally, trust your senses by sampling scents in-store or reading product descriptions to find the perfect match.

5 WAYS TO TAKE CARE OF YOUR CANDLES

Trim the wick to 1/4 inch before each use to prevent excessive smoke and uneven burning. Store candles away from direct sunlight and drafts to maintain their color and fragrance quality.
